1. Project Overview

Purpose
spaKo is a modern web platform that combines:

• Language Translation

• Language Learning

• Vocabulary Building

• Grammar Practice

• Pronunciation Training

• AI Chat Learning

• User Progress Tracking

The concept is based on a smart multilingual parrot that can translate and teach languages 
interactively.
